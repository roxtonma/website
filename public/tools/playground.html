Regex Tester

Regex flags modify how pattern matching works:

  • g (global): Find all matches, not just the first one
  • i (case insensitive): Ignore case differences when matching
  • m (multiline): ^ and $ match start/end of lines, not just whole string
  • s (dotall): Allow . to match newline characters

Example: /pattern/gi would match all occurrences (g) case-insensitively (i)
